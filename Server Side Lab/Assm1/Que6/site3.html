Central Park

Central Park is an urban park in Manhattan, located between the Upper West and Upper East Sides. It is the fifth-largest park in New York City, covering 843 acres (341 ha). The park was established in 1857 and is managed by the Central Park Conservancy under contract with the city. Central Park is a popular destination for both tourists and locals, with attractions such as the Central Park Zoo, the Central Park Conservatory Garden, and the Central Park Lake.
