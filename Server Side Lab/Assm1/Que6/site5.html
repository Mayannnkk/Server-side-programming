Times Square

Times Square is a major commercial intersection, tourist destination, and entertainment center in the Midtown Manhattan section of New York City. It is located at the junction of Broadway and Seventh Avenue and stretches from West 42nd to West 47th Streets. Times Square is known for its bright lights, billboards, and advertisements. It is also home to many theaters, including several Broadway theaters.
