Arts Department

The Arts department is one of the oldest and most respected departments in the college. Established in the year 1950, the department has been offering a wide range of courses in various disciplines of arts, including English, Sociology, History, and Philosophy. The department has a rich history of producing distinguished scholars, writers, and thinkers who have made significant contributions to their respective fields. The faculty members of the department are highly qualified and experienced, with many of them holding PhDs and other advanced degrees from reputed universities. They are dedicated to providing quality education to the students and helping them achieve their academic and career goals. The department also invites guest speakers and organizes seminars and workshops to provide students with exposure to the latest trends and developments in the field of arts. The Arts department is located in a spacious building with state-of-the-art classrooms, seminar rooms, and a well-equipped library. The library has a vast collection of books, journals, and other resources related to the various disciplines of arts. The department also has a dedicated computer lab with internet access to facilitate research and learning. In conclusion, the Arts department is a vibrant and dynamic department that offers quality education in various disciplines of arts. With its experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and diverse course offerings, the department is well-positioned to prepare students for successful careers in the field of arts.

English Course

The English course offered by the Arts department is a comprehensive program that covers various aspects of English language and literature. The course is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in English language skills, including reading, writing, speaking, and listening. It also introduces students to the rich and diverse world of English literature, from classical texts to contemporary works.
