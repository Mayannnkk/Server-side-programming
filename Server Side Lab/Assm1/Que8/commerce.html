Science Department

The Science department is one of the most popular departments in the college. Established in the year 1960, the department has been offering a wide range of courses in various disciplines of science, including Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. The department has a rich history of producing distinguished scholars, researchers, and scientists who have made significant contributions to their respective fields.

Computer Science

The Computer Science course offered by the Science department is a comprehensive program that covers various aspects of computer science and programming. The course is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science concepts, including algorithms, data structures, computer architecture, and software engineering. It also introduces students to the latest trends and developments in the field of computer science, such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cybersecurity.

Mathematics

The Mathematics course offered by the Science department is a comprehensive program that covers various aspects of mathematics and its applications. The course is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in mathematical concepts, including calculus, linear algebra, differential equations, and probability theory. It also introduces students to the latest trends and developments in the field of mathematics, such as mathematical modeling, data analysis, and computational mathematics.
